## Contracts Implemented

### 1. Calendar Tetris Optimization Engine (`calendar-tetris-optimization-engine.clar`)

**Purpose:** Implements Tetris-inspired algorithms to achieve maximum meeting density across organizational calendars.

**Key Features:**
- **Tetris-Style Slot Filling:** Algorithmic optimization ensuring zero idle time in calendars
- **Recursive Meeting Planning:** Automatic generation of meetings to plan future meetings
- **Stakeholder Synchronization:** Cross-functional alignment protocols with entropy-based scoring
- **Blockchain Verification:** Immutable meeting commitments with smart contract enforcement
- **Density Optimization:** Target 85%+ calendar utilization for maximum collaborative impact

**Core Functions:**
- `schedule-meeting`: Create blockchain-verified meeting slots with tetris scoring
- `optimize-daily-tetris`: Calculate and achieve optimal meeting density
- `execute-tetris-cascade`: Fill available slots with cascading meeting sessions
- `amplify-synergy-index`: Multiply collaboration factors across the organization

### 2. Action Item Entropy Generator (`action-item-entropy-generator.clar`)

**Purpose:** Generates actionable next steps that inherently require multiple clarification meetings for resolution.

**Key Features:**
- **Entropy-Driven Generation:** Scientific randomness ensures diverse action item complexity
- **Clarification Meeting Multiplication:** Minimum 3 meetings required per action item
- **Dependency Chain Creation:** Cross-functional requirements that cascade additional meetings  
- **Stakeholder Confusion Amplification:** Strategic complexity distribution for enhanced collaboration
- **Meeting Pipeline Sustainability:** Self-perpetuating action item ecosystem

**Core Functions:**
- `generate-action-item`: Create entropy-scored actions with built-in complexity
- `schedule-clarification-cascade`: Generate required follow-up meeting sequences
- `trigger-dependency-cascade`: Create cross-functional meeting dependencies
- `amplify-entropy-index`: Increase system-wide meeting generation rates
